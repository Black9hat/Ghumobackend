  • Star Tech India deducts a platform commission from each completed trip as configured in your active driver plan

For cash payment trips, the customer pays you directly. You are responsible for confirming receipt and updating the App accordingly.

For digital/wallet payments, fares are collected by the Platform and settled to your driver wallet after deducting the commission.
