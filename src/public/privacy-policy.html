Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 13, 2026

Welcome to Ghumo. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1 Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide you with the best ride-hailing experience:

a) Phone Number

  • Used for OTP-based login and account identification
  • Ensures secure authentication to your account

b) Location Information

We collect precise and approximate location data when:

  • You search for a ride
  • During active trips
  • For safety and ride tracking purposes

c) Ride Information

  • Pickup and drop locations
  • Ride history and patterns
  • Trip timestamps
  • Fare details and payment information

d) Device Information

  • Device type and model
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (for security & analytics)
  • Operating system information

2 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Match you with nearby drivers efficiently
  • Provide real-time ride tracking
  • Ensure ride safety for passengers and drivers
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Prevent fraud and misuse of our services
  • Communicate important updates about your rides
  • Provide customer support
Important: We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3 How We Share Information

We may share limited information in the following circumstances:

  • With drivers: Pickup & drop location during active rides only
  • With payment providers: Transaction processing (if applicable)
  • With legal authorities: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
Your Privacy Matters: We do not share your personal information for advertising purposes.

4 Data Storage and Security

  • All data is transmitted securely over HTTPS encryption
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security audits are conducted to ensure data protection

5 Data Retention

We retain your data:

  • As long as your account is active
  • As required by applicable laws and regulations
  • Until you request account deletion

6 Account Deletion

You have full control over your data:

  • You may request account deletion from within the app
  • Upon deletion, your account and associated data will be permanently removed
  • Some data may be retained for legal compliance purposes
  • Deletion requests are processed within 30 days

7 Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be reflected on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
